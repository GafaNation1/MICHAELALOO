Privacy Policy

How I collect, use, and protect your information.

Last updated: April 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Michael Odhiambo Aloo ("I", "me", "my") collects, uses, and protects information when you visit this portfolio website at michaelaloo.netlify.app. I respect your privacy and am committed to being transparent about how your data is handled.

Information I Collect

This website does not require you to create an account or log in. The information I may collect includes:

  • Name and email address, if you submit the contact form
  • Your message content, when you write to me directly
  • Basic usage data such as pages visited and time spent, collected through site analytics
  • Browser type and device information, collected automatically by the hosting platform

How I Use Your Information

Any information you provide is used only for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your enquiry or project request
  • To understand how visitors use the site so I can improve it
  • To communicate with you about a project, if we are working together

I do not use your information for advertising, and I do not sell or share it with third parties.

Cookies

This site may use basic cookies to support functionality and analytics. Cookies are small files stored on your device that help the site work correctly. You can disable cookies through your browser settings at any time. Disabling cookies will not affect your ability to browse the site.

Third-Party Services

This website is hosted on Netlify. Netlify may collect basic server logs including IP addresses as part of their infrastructure. This site also uses Google Fonts and Font Awesome, which are loaded from external CDN servers. These services have their own privacy policies and may collect limited usage data.

Data Security

I take reasonable steps to protect any information you submit through this site. The site is served over HTTPS to keep your connection secure. Contact form submissions are handled directly and are not stored on any public database.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to any personal data I hold about you
  • Ask me to delete your information
  • Withdraw consent at any time if you previously gave it

To exercise any of these rights, contact me directly using the details below.

Changes to This Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. I encourage you to review this page periodically.

Privacy Questions
